Our Classes

Join us for a variety of yoga classes suited for all levels:

  • Hatha Yoga: A gentle introduction to the most basic yoga postures.
  • Vinyasa Flow: A dynamic flow of poses that builds strength and flexibility.
  • Meditation: Guided sessions focusing on mindfulness and relaxation.
  • Restorative Yoga: A relaxing practice that uses props to support the body.
  • Pilates:A combination of approximately 50 simple, repetitive exercises to create muscular exertion
  • Zumba:One of the exercise programs that can provide a full-body workout and a dance class.
